Interactive Demo

Here's an interactive demo written with HTML5. To move the control points, click and drag them. To move the tangents, click and drag the area just outside the control points. By default, the curve keeps \(d_1\) and \(d_2\) equal, but you can also specify a custom \(d_1\) value below.
